## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a mathematician and computer scientist specializing in **Data Science** and **AI Engineering**. Currently based at the **Oxford University Clinical Research Unit (OUCRU)** in Ho Chi Minh City, my work lies at the intersection of computer science, public health, and epidemiology. 

I focus on developing innovative solutions that integrate advanced computational techniques to solve complex healthcare challenges, such as building robust data pipelines, edge-computing medical data acquisition, and deploying real-time predictive ML models.

* 🎓 **Education**: MSc in Computer Science & BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science (Vietnam National University, HCMUS).
* 💼 **Current Role**: Research Assistant at OUCRU (Emerging Infections & Molecular Epidemiology).
* 🌱 **Currently learning**: Advancing Transformer architectures for clinical data extraction.
* 💬 **Ask me about**: Full-stack data platforms (Django/Python), Clinical Statistics (R/Shiny), and Machine Learning deployment.
